Palmyra Palm Sap comes from the sweet sap that results from tapping the thick flowering stems of the Palmyra blossom. This sap is further vaporised at correct degree of temperatures, producing a raw glycaemic and nutrient-rich sugar syrup. Palmyra Palms have been traditionally tapped for their sweet nectar for centuries throughout the tropics, especially in the south part of Tamil Nadu in India and parts of Indonesia.

However, it is recently just catching on as an alternative natural sweetener that has many added benefits over chemically processed white sugar. Did you know that Palmyra Palm trees are one of the oldest living trees to produce flowers? So, in many ways this is an ancient source of sugar that has been in the nature for a very long time. When the blossoms are grown, the nectar is collected more systematically by climbing to the top of the Palmyra Palm trees for several times a day. The tapped sap, called "toddy", is slowly extracted over a period of time by tapping the end of a cut flower blossom stem. This pure blossom "juice" is commonly consumed fresh or fermented by native peoples in the India, Philippines, Indonesia and Malaysia. The Palmira sap is actually white in colour and a milky sweet liquid that has to be immediately processed (boiling at 100°F -120°F) to stop it from getting it fermented naturally. This makes the toddy a caramel-colour liquid syrup that is more concentrated in natural sugars.

Health Benefits A Low Glycemic Sweetener The Glycaemic Index (GI) is the standard measurement used to rank how fast blood sugar levels rise after we drink or eat a particular type of beverage or food over a 2 - 3 hour period of time. Concentrated sweeteners naturally have a higher GI compared to other foods. Sugars that are released slowly into the bloodstream are low glycaemic in nature and the most beneficial to consume on a regular basis.

  1. Glycemic Index Ratings
  2. Low Glycemic Index – 0-55
  3. Medium Glycemic Index – 56-69
  4. High Glycemic Index – 70-100

Low glycemic sweeteners are better choices for most people, but they are especially good for diabetics and those wanting to loose excess body weight. This is because they don't create blood sugar spikes and don't cause addictive eating behaviours and cravings soon afterwards. PalmSure is a Raw Sweetener, High in Enzymes.
